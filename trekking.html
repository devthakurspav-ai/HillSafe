Trekking Safety

For trekkers and adventure enthusiasts, safety is our priority. We provide:

  • 🗺️ Safe trekking routes in Himachal & Uttarakhand.
  • 📡 GPS-based emergency SOS for stranded trekkers.
  • 🚨 Alerts about landslides, heavy rains, and blocked trails.
  • ☎️ Helpline numbers for local rescue teams.

Example: In August 2024, our SOS feature helped rescue 12 trekkers stranded in Manali-Leh highway landslide.
