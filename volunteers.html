Volunteer Network

Join our volunteer team to help people in need. Volunteers can:

  • 📍 Share real-time disaster updates (floods, landslides, blocked roads).
  • 🚑 Help connect victims with emergency services.
  • 📦 Distribute food, water, and medical kits.
  • 📲 Use our mobile app for coordination.

Example: In July 2024, our volunteers provided emergency relief to flood-affected families in Kullu, Himachal Pradesh.
